This project was a combined capstone and district authority award project. I spent around a combined 130 hours creating this website and songs from scratch. Documentation of the journey is linked in the header. The audio player you see was built in JavaScript using the web audio API. I adapted it from a video by Before Semicolon on YouTube. The background and icons were animated using the anime.js where I created shapes in Adobe Illustrator and animated the SVG paths in JavaScript. The songs were all composed and arranged by me in Ableton Live Lite 11 as a journey of inspiration for creating art. This project let me discover the ways I like to create art and helped my diagnose where I find the biggest obstacles while allowing me to combine the two areas of creation I find the most enjoyable. Music and Computer Science.
